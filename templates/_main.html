Easily encrypt any file

Want to create a PDF only your friends can view? Want to keep a picture of your credit cards on Dropbox, but feel uncomfortable putting that on the internet?

Use PasswordProtectMyFile.com to protect any file with a password, without your file ever leaving your computer. All you need to decrypt the result is a web browser.

And the password, of course.
